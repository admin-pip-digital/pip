Collaboration & communication

Working cross-functionally, articulating design rationale, influencing without authority

Project: Mapping the Optavia Coach Experience

Problem

Product decisions were being made based on incomplete mental models rather than true understanding of user behavior. Teams were responding to surface-level requests like “make things look nice” instead of addressing real problems coaches experienced. Nobody fully understood the complex coach experience.

Approach

I took ownership of mapping the entire coach experience, facilitating weekly collaborative sessions with product managers to build shared understanding. This wasn’t traditional research - it was facilitative leadership through organizational complexity, making a mess together until patterns emerged.

Solution

Through persistent weekly sessions, we created a comprehensive coach experience sitemap that became the foundational document for all subsequent product decisions. The systematic understanding shifted focus from reactive design requests to strategic problem-solving.

Impact

The sitemap became referenced for every coach portfolio product decision going forward. This work was significant enough that it earned my promotion to Senior Designer - proving that sometimes the most valuable design happens before any interfaces are created.
