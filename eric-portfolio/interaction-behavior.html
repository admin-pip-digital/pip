Project: Optavia Health Journey Questionnaire

Problem

Optavia’s coach-dependent acquisition model couldn’t scale. New users arrived motivated but confused about which health program fit their needs, creating a bottleneck that limited growth.

Approach

We designed a smart questionnaire that educates users about their options while gathering information to route them properly. The insight: people don’t just need a program recommendation - they need to understand why it’s right for them.

Solution

An adaptive questionnaire that starts broad (goals, exercise habits) then gets specific based on responses, identifies specialized needs (diabetes, pregnancy, medication compatibility), routes users to appropriate programs while preserving coach relationships, and seamlessly hands off to partners when relevant.

Impact

The branching logic handles dozens of user scenarios while feeling simple. Users get personalized recommendations that make sense, coaches get better-prepared leads, and the business opened new pathways without losing what made them successful.
