Optavia health journey questionnaire

The Problem

Optavia's coach-dependent acquisition model couldn't scale. New users arrived motivated but confused about which health program fit their needs, creating a bottleneck that limited growth.

The approach

We designed a smart questionnaire that does two things at once: educates users about their options while gathering the information needed to route them properly. The key insight was that people don't just need a program recommendation - they need to understand why it's right for them.

The Solution

An adaptive questionnaire system that:

  • Starts broad (goals, exercise habits) then gets specific based on responses
  • Identifies specialized needs (diabetes, pregnancy, medication compatibility)
  • Routes users to appropriate programs while preserving coach relationships
  • Seamlessly hands off to LifeMD for weight loss medication when relevant

Why This Worked

The branching logic (shown in the flow diagram) handles dozens of user scenarios while feeling simple. Users get personalized recommendations that make sense to them, coaches get better-prepared leads, and the business opened new pathways without losing what made them successful.
