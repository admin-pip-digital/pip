Helping a Small Utah Spa Go From Struggling to Profitable

Project Overview

We designed and developed a marketing campaign for a local spa. This included updating their online presence, Google Business Profile, website, an ad campaign to gain attention for their grand opening.

62% increase

Launch timeline

The Challenge

Execute a complete digital transformation within one week on a startup budget. Demonstrate that professional-grade marketing infrastructure could be deployed quickly without sacrificing quality or strategic thinking.

Context

Time 4 U Spa, a new wellness business in our local market, opened with strong service offerings but zero digital presence. They needed immediate market entry to capitalize on their grand opening momentum.

The Strategy for Time 4 U Spa

We spent time in the beginning to deep dive with the owner to understand his business and goals. This is an important step to make sure we are strategizing with your needs and your budget and not applying a one-size-fits all solution.

We then defined everything that business needed (including an audit on the customer experience) and scaled down to what would make the biggest impact now in the shortest amount of time.

Our Approach

We delivered a comprehensive digital launch package in 3 business days:

  • Professional website with online booking integration
  • Analytics and conversion tracking setup
  • Social media profiles and content strategy
  • Targeted local ad campaigns on Facebook and Google
  • Brand messaging and positioning framework

Google Ads Launch Impact (July 6th)

  • Surge in "Book online" clicks after ads launch (3 to 53 clicks)
  • Increase in call clicks post-launch (1 to 42 clicks)
  • Jump in text clicks after ads went live (1 to 23 clicks)
  • Increase in address clicks following launch (2 to 27 clicks)

Website Traffic

  • Increase in active users after Google Ads launch (58 to 171)
  • Boost in new users post-launch (58 to 168)

Meta Ads Reach (June)

  • Reached 11,011 total people (5,604 males, 5,407 females)
