About me

I consider myself an expert at "figuring things out as I go" and "treating everyone with empathy and respect." I'm mission- and impact-driven and work on what actually makes a difference. I've been fortunate to see a lot of different types of success in my career, and I'm optimistic about leaving anything I touch better than I found it.

Most recently at Optavia, I reduced development cycles from 4 months to 2-3 weeks, mapped complex user experiences that became foundational product documents, and designed the strategic pivot from coach-dependent to client-led acquisition serving 800K+ users. My approach focuses on identifying systemic problems rather than treating symptoms - the best design work often happens before any interfaces are created.

I am currently experimenting with AI-integrated workflows at my agency while seeking focused product work where I can apply strategic systems thinking at scale.
